My company · Product stories

Existing cameras.
A clearer operating picture.

I started Octivis to turn video stores already collect into practical signals about queues, dwell, register demand, and preventable loss.

01 / 03
01 / Floor context

See the store as one operating system.

Octivis connects movement, queues, and service zones to the layout managers already understand.

Existing video → live floor context
02 / Coordinated intelligence

Turn isolated signals into one useful answer.

Specialized agents can compare what is happening across the floor and surface the next decision to the right person.

Multiple signals → one operating picture
03 / Earlier intervention

Act while there is still time to change the outcome.

Useful alerts help store teams respond to pressure points and suspicious patterns before they become larger problems.

Earlier context → faster intervention
