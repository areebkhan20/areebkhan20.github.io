See the money your store is losing.

Octivis is the AI perception layer for physical retail. It connects to the POS, books, and cameras stores already use to find the losses draining their margins—without biometric identification. Stores only pay from what Octivis recovers.

01 / Supplier recovery

Find supplier overcharges hiding in the books.

Octivis compares the records stores already keep so owners can catch invoice discrepancies and recover lost margin.

Books + invoices → recoverable loss
02 / Payment intelligence

Surface payment gaps before they become routine.

POS and transaction signals reveal card anomalies, missed payments, and register exceptions that would otherwise stay buried.

POS + payments → clear exceptions
03 / Physical loss

Detect fuel shrink and theft with existing cameras.

Non-biometric perception connects activity on the floor with store operations, helping teams investigate loss and act earlier.

Cameras + context → earlier action
